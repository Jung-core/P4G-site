Build back better and greener

2021 P4G Seoul Summit will be a prime opportunity to
strengthen the bond of the international society to
build back better and greener.

The Summit is a multilateral conference of world leaders gathering under the theme
‘Inclusive Green Recovery towards Carbon Neutrality’.
More than 60 Heads of State and leaders of international organizations will discuss Green Recovery, Carbon-Neutrality, Public-Private Partnership for Climate Action.
